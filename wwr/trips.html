Trip Locations

Heron Creek

The water is relatively calm and there are few twists and turns. Recommended for first time rafters.

Bull Creek

The rapids are strong and unpredictable, which makes it a good teaching tool. Just make sure to pay attention so you don't get washed out.

Serpent's Pass

Part of the challenge here is that there are spaces where you have to dock the raft and carry it with you on the land to get past certain obsticals.

Las Fauces del Diablo

The hardest of them all. Each raft can only hold a single passenger. Not for the faint of heart.

The Dart

What it lacks in winding turns and obsticals it makes up for by being really fast.
